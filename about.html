About Me

I was originally born from Argentina, raised in California and brought over to Florida about six years ago, where I ended up at the University of Florida in Spring 2012.

I knew I wanted to enter journalism when I realized, as a little girl, that instead of just pouring over all my mom's vintage Vogue's and Bazaar's, I could create magazines. Art has always been a part of me, while writing was a challenge I enjoyed doing.

I hope one day I can merge my two life-long loves of art and writing through design and editing.

Although most people have an oblique outlook on journalism's industry, I am excited to see where all the exciting innovations take us.
